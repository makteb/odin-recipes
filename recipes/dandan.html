Dan Dan Noodles

Recipe Description

Dan Dan Noodles – the iconic spicy Sichuan noodles, a flavour explosion with slippery noodles tossed in an intense sesame chilli sauce and pork. You’ll need to visit your local Asian grocery store but once you’ve gathered the ingredients, it’s a cinch to make!

Ingredients

  • Chili Oil
  • 3 teaspoons oil
  • 8 oz. ground pork (225g)
  • 2 teaspoons sweet bean sauce or hoisin sauce
  • 2 teaspoons shaoxing wine
  • 1 teaspoon dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon five spice powder
  • 1/3 cup sui mi ya cai
  • 2 tablespoons sesame paste
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon five spice powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sichuan peppercorn powder
  • 1/2 cup of your prepared chili oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • ¼ cup hot cooking water from the noodles
  • 1 pound fresh or dried white noodles
  • 1 small bunch leafy greens
  • chopped peanuts
  • chopped scallion

Recipe Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients except oil and chicken stock. Then gently stir in oil and stock – oil should be sitting on surface. Set aside.
  2. Mix together hoisin, soy, Chinese wine and five spice (“Sauce”).
  3. Heat oil in a skillet or wok over high heat. Add pork and cook, breaking it up as you, until it changes from pink to white. Add Sauce and cook for 1 minute, then transfer into a bowl.
  4. Return skillet to stove, reduce to medium heat. Add oil into middle of skillet.
  5. PAdd Sui mi ya cai and stir for 30 seconds, just to warm through. Set aside.
  6. Ladle 1/4 of Dan Dan Sauce into a bowl. Pile in noodles, top with pork and Sui mi ya cai. Place choi sum on side.
  7. Sprinkle with peanuts and green onions and serve.

Enjoy!
